Sleep at the Village

Sweet Dreams!

The Village provides two options to suit the different clientele that frequent the establishment.

Our ocean view bungalows make you wish you never have to leave and give you the true exclusive beach experience. Our beachfront bungalow is just as comfortable and features the same complete setup with a full bath, refrigerator, and bar.

Beach Life

Our bungalows sit beachfront with picture windows looking out onto the white sands and rolling blue tides of My Khe Beach.

The sunrise over clear water is just steps away in the morning and all day the picturesque jungles of Son Tra mountain, fixed with the white statue of Lady Buddha, look over the laidback beach life passing by.

Nightly Fun

As the sun sets, guests can return to the terrace to take in a cocktail or healthy smoothie and bask in the stars and cool night air.

The service here is attentive throughout the day and makes sure all wants and whims get quickly met. Whether you’re passing through Da Nang on a short stay or a long one, The Village guarantees a relaxed place to stay and take in the coastline at a rate any traveller can manage.

The Village | Da Nang

We provide the atmosphere of a top-tier resort—the professional cocktails, carefully prepared natural food, and top shelf service—for a reasonable price. Our mission is to ensure our visitors on this scenic slice of beach can count on a quality place to rest and a good time in the sand and sun.

At our unique beach venue you can expect:

Beachside Hostel

With our ocean view bungalows you’re guaranteed of an amazing beach experience in Da Nang.

Live Music

Musical events to relax our guests while the music drifts over the beanbags and hammocks, down to the white sands and the shade of the palms.

Beach Bar

Our lively BREEZE Bar with a terrace and comfy bean bags for the ultimate beach relaxation experience.

Beach Life

Summer Night Bonfires on the Beach, Frisbee’s, Fat Tyre Bike Rental and Volleyballs available for your enjoyment.

Food at The Village

 Our kitchen will suit any taste and serves up all manner of tasty food to satisfy your appetite.

Nourish

The Smoothies & Juices we have on offer all come fresh and packed with best nature has to offer, all made from fresh local produce.

ABOUT THE VILLAGE | DANG

The Village Guest House and Resort is a lively community of travelers, locals, and expats dedicated to celebrating life and leisure with our guests.

This unique venue seeks to marry the laidback and social experience of a guesthouse with the high quality accouterments, services, and ambiance of a beachfront resort.
