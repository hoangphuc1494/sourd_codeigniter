Our private rooms are an excellent choice for couples, small families, or simple travelers that prefer the luxury of the beach without the cost or isolation of a 5 star resort. Located on the second floor, these private bungalows feature a full bath, bar, refrigerator, WiFi and a private balcony overlooking the placid sea and white sand of My Khe Beach, Da Nang.

These rooms come with a special deal for travelers who stay longer: Stay 5 nights, pay for only 4!
If you want to get the most out of their stay at The Village | Da Nang, be sure to get this deal!

Relax
Our rooms feature furniture that is handmade locally with colonial style dark hardwood, they come equipped with 1 ultra comfortable king-sized & 1 luxurious twin-sized bed.
All of our bungalows include air conditioning, free WiFi, charging stations for laptops & mobile devices with a fully stock-able bar & fridge.

Enjoy
Fully equipped spacious bathroom, all expected comforts: toilet, shower, and a comfortably spacious 2-persons bathtub. We attend to all of your comforts & needs, striving to provide our Villagers with the ultimate holiday experience, with direct access to our pristine beach front… without breaking your budget!

Wake up at the pristine My Khe Beach at The Village | Da Nang!
Enjoy our unique beachfront venue; Drinks & Food, Live Music & DJ’s and the true Da Nang Beachlife.

Play
Children, (and pets) are most welcome at The Village | Da Nang, and with free parking on the property we take away the worries of managing these hard daily tasks, let the holidays commence!
Our operative children’s beach area combined with a functioning freshwater hand pump, will keep the young ones busy, while you can relish our private beach area and clear blue ocean, or a cool drink while shooting pool.

Nourish
Our Village Executive Chef Alberto from Napoli is a long term resident of Vietnam hailing from an Olive Farm located in the heart of Ferrandina, Italy. Next to other cooking skills, here Alberto learned the secrets of creating Oliva Majatica di Ferrandina. Alberto loves serving only the absolutely freshest & most flavorful ingredients with a true Italian flare for excellence!

Thirsty?
Simply step out your door to enjoy beverages from Breeze, our signature bar. We offer Fresh Juices & Smoothies, Cocktails, Beer and Spirits of all kinds.

Da Nang’s Villagers never go hungry or thirsty… Team Village is here for you!

Comfort!
Our beachfront bungalows are The Village’s premiere offering. Wake up for the sunrise on the beach, brew a cup of Vietnamese coffee, open the double doors of your private balcony & enjoy the sea breeze with rhythms of the ocean and spend your lazy day on one of our wooden sun decks or with one of our signature beanbags on the pristine & World Famous My Khe Beach!
